About Mike Morkes

Throughout my career, I have been a self-starter. A strong dose of curiosity often leads me to jumping into learning new concepts and technologies. Whether it’s learning a new technique for programming in HTML and JavaScript, utilizing a new design tool, or finding new ways to more efficiently use my computer and software, I’m always researching and implementing online training solutions. My willingness to learn a new piece of software or a new way to code has stretched my abilities, and made me a more valuable asset companies can leverage for new revenue opportunities.

Because of my years of design experience, plus my training and on-the-job use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I am a unique fit for companies that need an employee who can adapt quickly to new scenarios in both UI/UX as well as front end development.

If you hire me, you will be adding a team member with years of web, interactive, and print design experience, coupled with a desire to always push myself and learn more.
