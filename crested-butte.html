Equipment Rentals:

OKC Ski Club gets a 30% discount. Go here to reserve.

Food:

They pay for dinner on the way back, you are on your own for all other meals. A bus runs from the condo to a grocery store if we want to cook our own meals. I'm willing to cook if you pitch in for groceries. I know there's one night that the club is eating out at a Mexian restaurant. One meal I was thinking about making was chili. I would add Baked Potato soup to that list, but that would require a crockpot...doubt the condo has one.

Bus & Parking

Bus leaves from Will Rogers Airport Off-Airport lot #1. Rate is $5/day.

Bus is not train car style, so we'll have to share bunks and spoon. You can drink on the buses (in moderation). They supply soft drinks, ice, water and cups, so if you want to drink on the bus, I'm guessing easiest thing to do is bring a small bottle of your favorite booze and mix from their drinks. If the bus doesn't have a bathroom on board, there will likely only be one or two bathroom stops, so plan accordingly on your drinks!

Condo

She is going to try and assign us a 2-bedroom condo so we can raise a little hell without bothering other club members.
