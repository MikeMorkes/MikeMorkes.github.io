Limousin World website, 2010

Limousin World is the official breed publication of the North American Limousin Foundation, serving enthusiasts of the Limousin breed of cattle in the United States and Canada. The goals of this project were to keep the menu options simple while offering a variety of information on the front page in a easy-to-view layout. Keeping an appropriate look and feel for the demographic was a high priority as well. I did all development on this project as well, which included HTML, CSS, and Javascript; I then converted the site to a Wordpress theme.

Bedlam Nation social website, 2008

Social website based around the in-state rivalry between Oklahoma Sooners fans and Oklahoma State Cowboys fans. Depending on which side you chose, when logged in, your UX would use the appropriate school colors and photos on all Bedlam Nation pages. While the site initially had a strong start, the emergence of Facebook overtook most specialized social sites like this one, and bedlamnation.com was shuttered in 2010.

2014 ICF Canoe Marathon rich media digital ad

A rich media ad promoting the sale of tickets to the 2014 IFC Canoe Marathon at the OKC Boathouse District. Ad was animated from a single action photo of a previous race with use of some support image pieces, such as splashing water, to give the illusion of a slow-mo, parallax video. Each moving piece (arms, paddles, splashes, foreground, and background) was separated from the source photo; touched up as necessary; imported into Flash, and then animated in object groups.
