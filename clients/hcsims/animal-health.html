Animal Health and Management

 

  • Vaccine & Routine Care Protocol
    • All cattle are vaccinated annually for Blackleg, IBR, BVD, PI3, BRSV, Lepto, and Pastuerella.
    • Cattle are dewormed twice per year with a rotation of Eprinex and Dectomax.
  • Nutrition
    • HC Sims Farms has begun to use organic fertilizer to enrich the mineral content of the soil and grass to improve cattle nutrition.
    • All of our hay is either grown on the farm in PA or here locally in Mercer County, KY.
  • Herd Testing
    • We have one of the strictest herd health testing programs in the country of any breed, we test annually for BLV, Anaplasmosis and Johnes. We also test each animal once in their lifetime for BVD PI.
  • Genetic Testing
    • Each animal that has either unknown status or could be a possible carrier for hypotrichosis is tested. We know the status of each animal on the farm and have taken the guesswork out of it for our customers.
