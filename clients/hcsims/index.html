Who We Are

For over 80 years, the Sims family has harvested the land and developed livestock in the hills of Western Pennsylvania. Now with an expanding purebred operation in Central Kentucky, under the leadership of Ms. Ellen Sims, they are aiming to produce world-class Belted Galloway genetics, provide industry leading customer service and assist in the vital preservation of the breed and the environment.

What We Produce

BULLS

HC Sims Farms raises a limited number of quality bulls annually who meet our strict selection criteria for phenotypic quality, reproductive traits, and disposition. Bulls are available off the farm or periodically through public auction.

SEMEN

Many leading A.I. sires are available through HC Sims Farms, we invite you to contact us and we can visit about the best breeding options for you.

SHOW HEIFERS

Several of the very finest show cattle in the breed are represented in our genetic base; consequently we have several elite show heifer and show steer prospects available each year.

REPLACEMENT FEMALES

As our herd develops and we reach capacity we will be offering several bred cows, cow/calf pairs and replacement heifers for sale.
